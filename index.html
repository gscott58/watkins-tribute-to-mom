On the Day of Mom's Funeral . . .

As I glanced out my window, River Road is where I be, I see many trees and the Desplaines River across the street.   I thought to myself, my mother's love is here with me.   As I sat in silence and wiped away my tears, what do I hear?   My mother is singing her favorite song, it is ringing in my ears.... "One more River to Cross".

Oh how wonderful God is to send me this peace and assurance that she has made it across.   Her mom, dad, sister, daughter, grandsons, son-in-laws were there, but they couldn't help her cross. God standing with his wide-open arms, was the only one who helped her cross.

I love you mom, God has carried you home.   You made it over to the other side of life, no more rivers for you to cross. Your motherly love will always live in our hearts.


Your Daughter,

Christine
